Mr. Coffee, our
commitment begins in coffee.



We could not offer a space of freedom of expression and
sharing without thinking of what we serve in our cups.

All our coffees come from fair trade farms: farmers receive
fair remuneration for their labor, working conditions respect
human rights.

This is what makes our coffees so good: they grow, are
grown, picked, and dried with respect and love.

Mr. Coffee, a coffee (almost)
like the others.



Here, we come to find someone, or to be with us, to work
on our computer or to relax, to read among the books in
our library or simply to enjoy a good coffee.

All this different world that meets without seeing each
other, it made us want to do something more.

This is how the philosopher Mr. Coffee was born.

Our ambition: to bring together people from all walks of
life around social issues, philosophical debates, their
common passion for writing and letters.
